Email Hasher

This page makes no external HTTP requests at all, not to hash the emails, nor to load any kind of scripts. Feel free to view source to confirm. No data is stored in any way and no usage is tracked.

The method here is roughly: MD5(LOWER(TRIM(email))). Emails are made lowercased and trailing spaces are removed before hashing.
